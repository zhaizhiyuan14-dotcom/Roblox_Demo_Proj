Descend & Ascend

A Roblox horror-adventure game where players must escape the Lower Levels and reach Heaven.

Fight your way through dangerous underground areas filled with hostile NPCs, deadly obstacles, collapsing structures, and brutal environments. Every level becomes harder as stronger enemies and new challenges appear.

Features
Horror survival gameplay
Fast-paced movement
Aggressive enemy NPCs
Parkour and obstacle sections
Multiple world layers
Dark atmospheric environments
Final ascension to Heaven
Gameplay

Players begin trapped in the Lower Levels with only one objective:

Climb upward and survive.

Throughout the journey, players will encounter:

Hostile humanoid enemies
Monstrous entities
Dangerous terrain
High-risk jumps and movement sections
Controls
Action	Key
Move	WASD
Jump	Space
Sprint	Shift
